Welcome!

Maui Mugs Coffee Shop is home grown and all natural, based in the beautiful island of Maui in Hawaii. What better way to wake up and grab a cup of joe than from an authentic, beach front Hawaiian favorite? On your trip to the island, it's a disservice to yourself to not stop by. Join the locals and experience authentic Hawaiian culture while enjoying the best coffee on the coast.
